Customer Reviews

Ajith Kumar @ajith kumar

"The food was absolutely delicious! The flavors were authentic, and every dish was cooked to perfection. The ambiance is cozy, making it a great place for a relaxed dinner. The staff were friendly and attentive, which made the whole experience even better. Highly recommend the Dosa, it was a standout!"

Soundarya Nanjundan @Soundarya Nanjundan

"I had high expectations, but the restaurant didn't quite live up to them. While the menu had a good variety, the dishes we tried were a bit bland and lacked seasoning. The service was also a bit slow, even though the restaurant wasn't too busy. The atmosphere is nice, but overall, it was an average dining experience."

Vijay @vijay

"This is hands down one of the best restaurants I've been to! The presentation of each dish was stunning, and the flavors were perfectly balanced. The staff was very knowledgeable about the menu and offered great recommendations. It's a bit pricey, but totally worth it for a special occasion."

Pavithra Janani @Pavithra

"The restaurant has a trendy vibe, and the decor is very stylish. However, the noise level was quite high, which made it hard to have a conversation. The food was good but didn't blow me away. The service was prompt, but I felt like we were being rushed to free up the table. It's a decent spot, but not sure I’d rush back."
